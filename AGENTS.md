## Study notes (`NoteDraft`)

When authoring or editing note copy under `lib/notes/` (draft modules, future CMS, etc.), follow **`lib/notes/AUTHORING.md`** for required structure, depth, Cambridge-aligned voice, and integrity rules so every subtopic stays consistent.

## Practice hub — structured drills

When authoring or editing **subtopic practice packs** in `lib/practice/*-practice.ts`, follow **`lib/practice/STRUCTURED_DRILLS.md`**. The bar is **non-negotiable**: the **10 drills** in each pack must, **together**, cover **every** learning outcome for that subtopic (all **definitions** and all **calculations** the syllabus asks for, using the per-subject learning-outcome files under `data/syllabus/`), and model answers must stay **high-detail, exam register**. The doc is the source of truth for the UI field mapping, coverage workflow, and checklists.
